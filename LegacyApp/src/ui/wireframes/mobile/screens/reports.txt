INTERACTIONS
================================================================================

1. Tap time period dropdown -> Shows date range options
2. Tap [Export] -> Shows export options
3. Tap chart/card -> May show detailed breakdown
4. Tap tag in list -> Could filter by that tag
5. Tap [<] back -> Returns to Menu
