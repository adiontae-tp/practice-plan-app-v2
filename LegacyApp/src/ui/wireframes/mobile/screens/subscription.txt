RESTORE PURCHASES
================================================================================

[Restore Purchases] link behavior:
1. Shows loading indicator
2. Checks app store for purchases
3. Success: "Purchases restored!"
4. No purchases: "No purchases found"
5. Error: "Unable to restore. Try again."


MANAGE SUBSCRIPTION
================================================================================

[Manage Subscription] button:
- Opens device's subscription management
  - iOS: App Store subscription settings
  - Android: Google Play subscription settings
- Only shown for active subscribers


INTERACTIONS
================================================================================

1. Tap "View details [>]" -> Opens plan-detail modal
2. Tap [Restore Purchases] -> Restores previous purchases
3. Tap [Manage Subscription] -> Opens native subscription manager
4. Tap [Upgrade/Subscribe] in modal -> Initiates purchase flow
5. Tap [<] back -> Returns to Menu


PLAN TIERS SUMMARY
================================================================================
