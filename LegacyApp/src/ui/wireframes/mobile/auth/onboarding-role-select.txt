CONTINUE BUTTON
================================================================================

+------------------------------------------+
|                          [Continue]      |
+------------------------------------------+

- Right-aligned in footer
- Primary color
- Disabled until role selected
- Navigates to next step


INTERACTIONS
================================================================================

1. Tap role card -> Select that role
2. Tap [Continue] -> Navigate to sport-select
3. Only one role can be selected at a time


ROLE DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

HEAD COACH:
- Full admin access
- Can create/edit all content
- Can manage team settings
- Can invite other coaches
- Subscription owner

ASSISTANT COACH:
- Limited access based on permissions
- Can view and possibly edit practices
- Cannot manage team settings
- Invited by head coach


NEXT STEP
================================================================================

After selecting role and tapping Continue:
-> Navigate to /(auth)/onboarding/sport-select

Role is stored temporarily until onboarding completes
