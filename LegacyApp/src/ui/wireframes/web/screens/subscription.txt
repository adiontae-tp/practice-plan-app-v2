================================================================================

+----------------------------------------------------+
| [spinner] Initializing purchases...                |
+----------------------------------------------------+

Or error state:
+----------------------------------------------------+
| [!] Unable to load subscription info. Tap to retry |
+----------------------------------------------------+


INTERACTIONS
================================================================================

1. Click "View details >" -> Open plan detail modal
2. Click [Upgrade to X] in modal -> Initiate purchase
3. Click [Restore Purchases] -> Restore previous purchases
4. Click [Manage Subscription] -> Open native subscription manager
5. Click [Manage] in banner -> Open subscription settings


PLAN TIERS
================================================================================

FREE ($0/month):
- 3 practice plans
- 2 templates
- Basic features
- 1 coach

PRO ($9.99/month) - MOST POPULAR:
- Unlimited practice plans
- Unlimited templates
- All features
- Up to 5 coaches
- Priority support

TEAM ($24.99/month):
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited coaches
- Custom branding
- API access
- Dedicated support


BUTTON STATES IN MODAL
================================================================================

Non-subscriber viewing Free:
[Current Plan] - disabled, gray

Non-subscriber viewing Pro:
[Subscribe to Pro] - primary, blue

Pro subscriber viewing Team:
[Upgrade to Team] - primary, blue

Team subscriber viewing Pro:
[Switch to Pro] - secondary, outline
