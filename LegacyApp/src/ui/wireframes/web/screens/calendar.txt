Day Cell States:
- Default: Gray text on white
- Today: Blue circle around number
- Selected: Blue background highlight
- In selected week: Light blue tint
- Has practices: Blue dots below number (1-3 dots, or count for 4+)

Click behavior:
- Single click selects day, updates week list on right
- Also highlights the entire week containing that day

LOADING STATE
================================================================================

Full page skeleton:
- Calendar grid with skeleton cells
- Week list with skeleton day headers and cards


INTERACTIONS
================================================================================

Calendar Panel:
1. Click any day -> Selects day, shows that week in list
2. Click [<]/[>] -> Navigate months
3. Days in selected week get light highlight

Week List Panel:
1. Click [<]/[>] -> Navigate weeks, updates calendar selection
2. Click day header -> Selects that day in calendar
3. Click practice card -> Opens plan detail modal
4. Click [...] menu -> Shows dropdown with options
5. Click [+ Add] -> Opens plan/new modal with selected date

Header:
1. Click [+ New Practice] -> Opens plan/new modal


RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

Desktop (>=1024px):
- Full split view: Calendar ~60% width, Week list ~360px fixed
- Both panels fill viewport height (no internal scroll needed)
- Calendar rows distribute evenly based on month (5-6 rows)

Tablet (768px-1024px):
- Stacked layout: Calendar on top, Week list below
- Calendar shows full width, condensed height
- Week list full width, scrollable if needed

Below 768px:
- Switches to CalendarMobile component
