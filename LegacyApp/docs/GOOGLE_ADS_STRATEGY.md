# Google Ads Campaign Strategy - Practice Plan App

## Executive Summary

**Product**: Practice Plan App - Practice planning and team management for coaches
**Target Audience**: Coaches (youth sports, high school, college, club teams)
**Goals**: App installs, web signups, brand awareness
**Recommended Monthly Budget**: $1,500 - $5,000 (starting)

---

## Campaign Structure Overview

| Campaign Type | Goal | Platform Focus | Budget % |
|--------------|------|----------------|----------|
| App Campaigns (UAC) | App Installs | iOS & Android | 40% |
| Search Campaigns | Web Signups | Google Search | 35% |
| Performance Max | Multi-goal | All Google Properties | 15% |
| YouTube/Display | Brand Awareness | YouTube, GDN | 10% |

## Landing Page Recommendations

### For Search Ads (Web Signups)
- **URL**: practiceplanapp.com/coaches or /signup

- **CTA**: "Start Free" / "Create Your First Plan"
- **Trust Signals**: "10,000+ coaches", app store ratings, testimonials
- **Features**: Show 3-4 key features with icons

### For App Campaigns
- Optimize app store listings (ASO)
- Strong screenshots showing key features
- Video preview in app store
- Ratings/reviews are critical

## Conversion Tracking Setup

### Track These Events
1. **Sign-up** (Primary - web)
2. **App Install** (Primary - app campaigns)
3. **Free Trial Start** (Secondary)
4. **Subscription Purchase** (Primary value event)
5. **First Practice Plan Created** (Engagement)

### Attribution
- Use data-driven attribution if available
- Otherwise: Position-based (40/20/40)
- Conversion window: 30 days click, 1 day view

## Seasonal Considerations

### Peak Seasons (Increase Budget 50-100%)
- **August-September**: Fall sports start (football, soccer, volleyball)
- **November-December**: Winter sports start (basketball, hockey, wrestling)
- **February-March**: Spring sports start (baseball, softball, lacrosse, track)

### Off-Peak (Reduce Budget 25-50%)
- **June-July**: Most school programs on break
- **December holidays**: Lower coaching activity

### Opportunities
- Target "back to school" and "season prep" messaging
- Sport-specific campaigns timed to their seasons

## A/B Testing Roadmap

### Month 1-2
- Test headlines: Feature-focused vs. benefit-focused
- Test CTAs: "Start Free" vs. "Try Free" vs. "Sign Up Free"

### Month 3-4
- Test landing pages: Short vs. long form
- Test audiences: Custom intent vs. affinity

### Month 5-6
- Test bidding strategies: Target CPA vs. Maximize Conversions
- Test ad formats: RSA variations
